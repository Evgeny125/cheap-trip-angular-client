Agra

Agra, the city of love, is a budget traveller's paradise. This city is a perfect blend of culture, traditions, and architecture that will leave you mesmerized. The city is famous for its iconic Taj Mahal, which is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Agra is also home to several other historical monuments, including the Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri, which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The city is known for its vibrant festivals, including the Taj Mahotsav, which celebrates the rich culture and traditions of the city. The art and craft of Agra are also famous worldwide, with intricate marble inlay work and leather goods being the most popular. The national food of Agra is Mughlai cuisine, which is a blend of Indian and Persian flavors. The city is also famous for its sweet delicacies, including Petha and Gajak. Agra is full of surprises, and one of the most intriguing facts is that the Taj Mahal changes color depending on the time of day. Another interesting fact is that the Agra Fort was built by three different Mughal emperors, each adding their unique touch to the structure. For budget travellers, Agra offers several free or low-cost attractions, including the Mehtab Bagh, which offers a stunning view of the Taj Mahal, and the Jama Masjid, which is one of the largest mosques in India. The city also has several bustling bazaars, where you can shop for souvenirs and local handicrafts. In conclusion, Agra is a city that will leave you spellbound with its rich culture, traditions, and architecture. It is a perfect destination for budget travellers who want to experience the best of India without breaking the bank. So pack your bags and get ready to explore the city of love!
