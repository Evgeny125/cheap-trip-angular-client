Main attractions in London

British Museum

The British Museum is a world-renowned institution that houses a vast collection of art, artifacts, and archaeological treasures from various civilizations and time periods.
Big Ben

Big Ben is one of the most iconic landmarks in London, known for its magnificent clock tower and historic significance.
Tower of London

The Tower of London is a historic castle that has served various purposes over the centuries. It offers a glimpse into London's medieval history and houses the Crown Jewels.
Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace is the official residence of the British monarch in London. It is a symbol of the British monarchy and an architectural marvel.
